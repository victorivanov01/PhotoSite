About Me

Hey, I'm Victor, a 20 year-old, self-taught hobby photographer from Sofia, Bulgaria. As a photographer I started out with the urge to tell stories, give people new perspectives and ways of seeing the world. Nothing intrigues me more than capturing emotion, geometry, color and light into a precious vortex called time.

Viewers need to be transported, and my photos achieve that through careful compositions of color and tone. This is the end result of a process by which I bring the creative brief to life and make the subject part of a story.

what am i doing?

  • To me, photography is an art of observation. It’s about finding something interesting in an ordinary place… I’ve found it has little to do with the things you see and everything to do with the way you see them.
